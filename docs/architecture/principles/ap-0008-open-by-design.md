# Status of This Document

Working Draft.

---

# Principle

Ntangl SHALL define open, vendor-neutral specifications that enable interoperability across manufacturers, organisations and deployment environments.

---

# Rationale

Closed ecosystems create unnecessary integration cost, reduce competition and inhibit innovation.

Open standards allow participants to collaborate without requiring proprietary agreements or vendor-specific implementations.

---

# Implications

Protocol specifications SHOULD:

- be publicly documented
- be implementable without proprietary dependencies
- support multiple implementations
- avoid unnecessary vendor assumptions

Compliance SHALL be determined through conformance to published specifications rather than implementation origin.

---

# Non-Goals

Open by Design does NOT require:

- open-source implementations
- unrestricted access to private systems
- disclosure of proprietary algorithms
- disclosure of confidential operational data

Participants remain free to implement Ntangl using commercial or proprietary software provided interoperability is preserved.
